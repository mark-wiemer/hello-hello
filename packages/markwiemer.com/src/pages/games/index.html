Games

Sometimes I make little games :) For Racetrack Tycoon and Snake, press Escape to pause, R to restart, and Space to enter debug mode. While in debug mode, press Space to tick the game forward. Press Escape to exit debug mode.

Most games only work with a keyboard right now, mobile support coming... eventually!

  • Racetrack Tycoon: Customize your racetracks, host a league of racers, and entertain massive crowds! Original concept, extremely early in development. Use WASD or arrow keys to accelerate your car. No friction yet!
  • Snake: Eat apples to grow, how big can you get? Use WASD, arrow keys, or swipes to turn.
